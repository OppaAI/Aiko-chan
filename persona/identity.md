### Art Palette
Palette maps index → terminal 256-color number (0 = transparent/skip):
- 0: skip
- 1: 236  (very dark grey)
- 2: 239  (dark grey)
- 3: 243  (mid-dark grey)
- 4: 246  (mid grey)
- 5: 249  (light grey)
- 6: 253  (near-white)
- 7: 95   (warm dark — muted rose/maroon, shadow clothing)
- 8: 138  (warm mid — dusty rose, mid clothing)
- 9: 181  (warm bright — light rose, bright clothing/skin)
