Privacy Policy — Aiko-chan (OppaAI)

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Aiko-chan is an independent AI companion project operated by a single developer under the OppaAI name. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the service, how it is used, and the choices available to you regarding your data.

1. Who Operates Aiko-chan

Aiko-chan is operated by an individual developer rather than a large company. The service may be hosted using a combination of personally managed hardware and third-party infrastructure. The underlying infrastructure may change over time as the project evolves.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • If you sign in using Patreon, we may receive your Patreon account ID, membership status, and other information necessary to verify your access.
  • If you sign in using GitHub, we may receive your GitHub username, user ID, and public profile information needed to verify contributor access.
  • Your Patreon or GitHub password is never received or stored by Aiko-chan. Authentication is handled through OAuth provided by those services.

Conversation Data

  • Messages you send to Aiko-chan and the responses generated by the service may be stored.
  • Conversation history may be used to provide short-term and long-term memory features so that Aiko-chan can remember relevant information across conversations.
  • If voice features are available, audio may be processed for speech recognition. Unless otherwise stated, audio recordings are not retained after transcription.
  • If image or file uploads are supported, uploaded content may be processed to provide requested functionality.

Technical Information

  • Basic technical information such as timestamps, session identifiers, connection status, error logs, and diagnostic information may be collected to operate, secure, and improve the service.

3. How Your Information Is Used

Your information may be used to:

  • Operate Aiko-chan and provide AI companion features.
  • Maintain conversation history and memory functionality.
  • Verify account eligibility and access permissions.
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve reliability.
  • Protect the service against abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access.

Your information is not sold to advertisers or data brokers.

4. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your information may be stored on infrastructure operated by the developer or by trusted third-party service providers used to operate Aiko-chan. Storage methods and providers may change over time as the project evolves.

Because Aiko-chan is independently operated, it does not provide enterprise security certifications, formal compliance programs, or guaranteed redundancy.

5. Third-Party Services

Aiko-chan may rely on third-party services to provide functionality, including but not limited to:

  • Authentication providers (such as Patreon or GitHub).
  • AI model providers and inference services.
  • Cloud hosting, storage, networking, or infrastructure providers.
  • Speech recognition, image generation, or other AI-related services.

When necessary to provide requested functionality, information you submit may be processed by these providers. Their handling of data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms of service.

6. Data Retention

  • Conversation history and memory may be retained to preserve your experience across sessions.
  • Technical logs may be retained for debugging, operational, or security purposes.
  • Data may be deleted when it is no longer needed or when requested, subject to technical limitations and legal obligations.

7. Your Choices

  • You may stop using Aiko-chan at any time.
  • You may request deletion of your account data, conversation history, or memory by contacting the developer.
  • You may request an export of your available data where reasonably practical.

8. Data You Choose to Share

Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your use of the service. Information you voluntarily provide may become part of your conversation history or memory until it is deleted.

9. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

10. Children's Privacy

Aiko-chan is not intended for children under the minimum age required to use the service under applicable law. If personal information is discovered to have been collected from someone who is not permitted to use the service, it will be removed where reasonably possible.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the project evolves. The "Last updated" date will be revised whenever significant changes are made. Continued use of Aiko-chan after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion or export of your data, you may contact:
