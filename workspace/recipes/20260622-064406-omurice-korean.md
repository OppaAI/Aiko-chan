Classic Korean omurice (omelette rice) recipe: 

**Key ingredients:**
- Fluffy rice (preferably Japanese short-grain or jasmine)
- 2–3 eggs (for a thick omelette layer)
- Pork belly or beef (common protein)
- Carrot, onion, scallion (optional veggies)
- Ketchup, soy sauce, tonkatsu sauce, honey, water, milk (for sauce)

**Steps:**
1. Cook rice, mix with sauce ingredients (ketchup + soy sauce + tonkatsu + honey + water + milk).
2. Fry pork/beef until crispy, then cool.
3. Whisk eggs, pour over rice, and fold gently.
4. Wrap rice in omelette, pan-fry until golden.

**Notes:**
- Sauce balances sweet/savory.
- Omelette layer adds richness.
- Optional: add crispy veggies for texture.
