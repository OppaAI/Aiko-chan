**Sample Recipe: Peanut Butter Cornflake Cookies**
*Ingredients:* Peanut butter, cornflake cereal, butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract.
*Procedure:* Mix dough, roll into balls, flatten, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 10–12 minutes.

**Sample Recipe: Brown Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies**
*Ingredients:* Flour, brown butter, chocolate chips, leavening agent.
*Procedure:* Brown butter first, mix dough, bake at 375°F (190°C) for 10–12 minutes.
