Ingredients:
- Tart shell:
  - 21 or 22 honey graham crackers or digestive biscuits (10½ oz / 300 g)
  - ½ cup (115 g) unsalted butter, melted
  - 2 Tbsp honey
  - 2 Tbsp granulated sugar
  - ¼ tsp salt

- Custard:
  - 2 cups (480 g) milk
  - ½ cup (100 g) granulated sugar
  - 4 large eggs
  - 2 tsp vanilla extract

Preparation:
1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Mix graham crackers, melted butter, honey, sugar, and salt. Press firmly into tart pans.
3. Bake crust for 10–12 minutes, then cool.
4. Whisk milk, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth. Pour into cooled crusts.
5. Bake custard for 25–30 minutes until set. Cool before filling with fruit.

Flavor customizations:
- Replace vanilla with orange blossom water, rose water, or almond extract.
- Use any fruit: stone fruit, berries, pears, figs, or tropical fruits like bananas or kiwis.
