Classic French Fruit Tart

Ingredients:
- 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, sifted
- ½ cup European-style unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
- 3 tbsp granulated sugar
- 1 large pasture-raised egg yolk
- 2 tbsp ice water
- 1 cup whole milk
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 3 large pasture-raised egg yolks
- ¼ cup granulated sugar
- 2 tbsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
- 2 cups assorted seasonal berries (raspberries, blueberries, sliced strawberries)
- ¼ cup apricot preserves, warmed and strained

Preparation:
1. Mix flour, butter, 3 tbsp sugar, egg yolk, and ice water in a food processor until dough forms.
2. Knead gently, wrap, and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
3. Roll dough into a tart pan, prick bottom, and bake at 375°F for 23 minutes (weights first, then shell).
4. Cool shell completely before filling with pastry cream.
5. Whisk egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch; temper with hot milk and cream.
6. Add vanilla, fill tart, and top with berries/apricot preserves.
